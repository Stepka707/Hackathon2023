Building Brighter Futures for Orphaned Girls in India

We are a dedicated and efficient, small nonprofit committed to empowering orphaned girls in Andhra Pradesh, India. Our primary focus is on providing education, basic healthcare, safety, and daily meals for more than 135 girls, opening doors to freedom from societal challenges.

Support Beyond Grade 10: Our mission doesn't stop at grade 10. We actively guide girls into grade 11 and 12, vocational courses, junior college, or higher education for life-changing empowerment where possible. Sponsor a girl today and make a long-term impact.

What We Do

Focused and needs-driven help means efficient resource allocation.

  • 135+ orphans

    Or more receive annual aid at the orphanage, or extended support into higher ed or vocational training for empowered futures.

  • $1.2/child/day

    Low daily cost covers all essentials for orphan girls: Food, shelter, basic health, and education at the orphanage; approx. $4.2/day for higher ed or vocational training.

  • For 9+ years

    Consistent, unwavering commitment to empowering India’s orphan girls.

  • Only 5% OPEX

    Around 5% overhead ensures around $950 of every $1,000 donation reach orphan girls..
